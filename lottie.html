This was the last piece of the puzzle. The squad had worked on the majority of the apps except for this.


As a part of the mobile squad, I worked closely with the designer from the web squad to work on research, alignment and consistency.


The Property Detail Page was a challenging yet rewarding project with lots of cross-function collaborations.



Research

Property Detail Page aka PDP is where users look for detailed information on a property to help them make an informed decision. It's where they would reach out and contact an agent. It’s an important page yet it hasn’t had much TLC in the past years.


We kicked off the project with some discovery and define workshops involving various stakeholders that would be affected by this project. After the activities, I went on with more focused research work and conduct user testings!

Issues
  • Improve the enquiry form. The existing enquiry forms were confusing and daunting and it can be seen from the high number of drop-offs.
  • The PDP doesn't always have all the information.
  • Houston, we have consistency problem! It’s not always a bad thing if it has a purpose. Apps are able to use more native elements and enrich the interactions. Consistency in this context is not just about the layout or "look and feel". The information on the page has different hierarchy, labels, CTAs and even different enquiry topic options.
User Testing & Findings

I conducted some user testing with 10 users, they looked at the current state of the PDP and also the prototype. It was an early prototype with "rich" data which in my hypothesis, would "anticipate" users need (so they don't need to leave the app environment). So our users like rich data and helpful extra information to help them make that decision! Hooray!


I also managed to get users that came in for testing, to do some card sorting. I found that users in buy journey and rent journey have different requirements. They prioritise different things. Users in early research phase and users in "ready-to-commit" phase have different needs too.

Based on these, I proposed we build a PDP with 2 different data hierarchy for buy and rent PDPs.

Challenges

There were many challenges and limitations in this project. I could not list them here but I can definitely talk you through it.


The business challenge is to ensure the differences in products do not have negative impact to our consumers or customers.

UI

The web squad designer and I did a quick audit on UI things we should clean up and aligned on. We worked on the shared elements together, these are button style, pills and other things that we share cross platforms.
