Realcommercial App

Realcommercial.com.au is one of the biggest commercial property portals in Australia. They have a website and native mobile apps. The project o revamp the mobile apps started in May 2016 and at this point, the apps had not been touched for +/- 3years.

So much to work on. It was an elephant.

We had a design sprint to kick off the project. The elephant was divided into features; Search/filters, Home, Saved Properties, Profile, News, Property Alerts.

Biggest Challenge

Search filter is the bread and butter of any listing site. So it was the work that took a good 6 months of this 12 months project.
